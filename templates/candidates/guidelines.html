CV Guide

Follow these professional CV guidelines to ensure compliance with VUT application standards.

download Download Example Template

Required CV Structure

  1. Personal Information Full name, contact details, email, phone, address, and professional headshot (for public roles).
  2. Professional Summary Brief overview of your experience and career goals.
  3. Work Experience List positions, employers, durations, and responsibilities.
  4. Education & Qualifications Degrees, certifications, and relevant training.
  5. Key Skills Include both technical and soft skills relevant to the job.
  6. Additional Information Achievements, references, or volunteer work.

BMA CV Tips

  • Tailor your CV to each specific position.
  • Keep your layout professional and ATS-friendly.
  • Use concise bullet points to summarize achievements.
  • Include measurable results where possible.
  • Proofread carefully before submitting.
